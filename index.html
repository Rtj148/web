Welcome to Al-Baha

Al-Baha is a city located in the southwest of Saudi Arabia and is considered one of the most important cities in the Kingdom in terms of tourism and agriculture. The city hosts various government offices and major commercial centers, in addition to several traditional markets, the most notable of which is the Thursday Market. The population of Al-Baha is approximately 339,174 people. The city is surrounded by numerous forests, including Raghadan Forest, Al-Shahba Forest, and Dar Al-Jabal Forest.
